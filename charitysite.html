Clean Water Foundation

Clean Water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries.

As we face a global pandemic, the health of families around the world relies on access to safe water and people like you.

Our Work

Today more than 30 million people around the world can turn on a tap and safely use a toilet because small, affordable loans empowered them to get access to safe water and sanitation at home.

We celebrate the lives transformed and realize there is more to be done as we continue our work in 13 countries around the world.
